Connecting Farmers

Oyo State Ejawa Our Fish Multipurpose Cooperative Society is a pioneering initiative aimed at transforming the agricultural landscape. It is a voluntary association aims to empower farmers to achieve financial freedom through their farming activities. We combine the expertise of IT professionals with the experience of farmers to create a synergy that maximizes productivity, sustainability, and profitability. For farmers, getting the right price for their crops can be the difference between a profitable harvest and a rotten one. In fact, just a one-cent mistake could result in up to several million naira in losses. Our Cooperative Society aims to make the buying and selling process easier for farmers with products and services such as finance, consulting and crop storage facilities.

How to Join...

Oyo State Ejawa Our Fish Multipurpose Cooperative Society

  • Learn About Us:

    Before applying for membership, we encourage you to learn more about Oyo State Ejawa Our Fish Multipurpose Cooperative Society and what we stand for. Explore our website, attend informational events, and reach out to current members to gain insights into our cooperative's mission, values, and membership benefits.

  • Review Membership Criteria

    Ensure that you meet the eligibility criteria for membership at Oyo State Ejawa Our Fish Multipurpose Cooperative Society. Our cooperative is open to individuals and businesses from various backgrounds and industries.

  • Complete Membership Application

    Once you're ready to join, complete our membership application form. Fill the application form from our website, office, or membership coordinator. Provide accurate and complete information about yourself or your business, including contact details, relevant experience, and any supporting documentation required.

  • Submit Application and Fees

    Submit your completed membership application along with any required fees or dues. The application process may involve a one-time membership fee and your desire share capital. Be sure to review the application instructions carefully and include all necessary payments to expedite the process.

  • Get Involved

    Congratulations! You're now a valued member of our cooperative community. Take advantage of the countless opportunities for networking, collaboration, and growth that come with membership. Get involved in committees, attend events, and contribute your skills and expertise to help advance our shared mission and goals.

  • Stay Connected

    Stay connected with Oyo State Ejawa Our Fish Multipurpose Cooperative Society and your fellow members by subscribing to our newsletter, following us on social media, and participating in online forums and discussions. Keep abreast of upcoming events, initiatives, and opportunities to maximize your membership experience and make the most of your cooperative journey.
